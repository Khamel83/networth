Frequently Asked Questions

How do I join the ladder? +

Click the "Join Ladder" button on the homepage and fill out the registration form. You'll need to provide your email, name, and self-assessed NTRP skill level. New players start at the bottom of the ladder.

How do I challenge someone? +

Log into your dashboard and select an opponent up to 3 positions above you. Send them a challenge through the system, and coordinate match details via email. The challenged player has 7 days to respond.

What if my opponent doesn't respond? +

If a player doesn't respond to a challenge within 7 days without a valid reason (illness, travel, etc.), you automatically move up one position. Contact support if you need to report an unresponsive player.

How do I report a match score? +

After your match, log into your dashboard and submit the score. Both players should submit scores within 48 hours. If scores don't match, contact support for dispute resolution.

What courts can we play at? +

You can play at any public court in East Side LA. Our approved courts include Vermont Canyon, Griffith Park, Echo Park, Silver Lake, Elysian Park, and Los Feliz Municipal. Both players must agree on the location.

Can I take a break from the ladder? +

Yes! Contact support to set your status to "inactive." You won't be challengeable, but you'll keep your current ranking. When you return, you may need to defend challenges from players now ranked above you.

I forgot my password. What do I do? +

Contact support with your registered email address and we'll help you reset your password.
