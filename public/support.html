Frequently Asked Questions

How do I join the ladder? +

Contact the league organizer to get added. Once you're in, you'll receive an email at the start of each month with your match pairing.

How do monthly matches work? +

At the start of each month, you'll be paired with another player at a similar skill level. You'll receive an email with your opponent's contact info and availability. Just hit reply to email them directly and coordinate a time to play.

What if I can't play this month? +

No problem! Before the 1st of the month, mark yourself as "unavailable" in your profile. You'll automatically be back in the pool the following month. Your ranking stays the same while you're away.

How do I report a match score? +

After your match, log into your dashboard and enter both set scores (e.g., 6-4, 3-6). Either player can report the score. You'll also be asked if you'd like to play this person again - this helps us make better future pairings.

What if we don't finish by month end? +

That's okay! Matches scheduled in December can be completed in January, February, or later. We'll send a friendly check-in reminder, but there's no penalty. Just report the score whenever you finish playing.

How does ranking work? +

Rankings are based on total games won, not match wins. Every game counts! A close 7-6, 6-7 match gives both players nearly the same credit. This rewards competitive play and keeps things fair even when you lose.

How do I log in? +

Just enter your email on the login page and we'll send you a magic link. No password needed! Click the link in your email and you're in.

What courts can we play at? +

Play at any approved East Side LA public court: Vermont Canyon, Griffith Park (Riverside or Merry-Go-Round), Echo Park, Hermon Park, Eagle Rock, Cheviot Hills, or Poinsettia Park.
