The System

How It Works

01

Get Paired

Each month, we pair you with another player at your skill level. You'll get an email with their contact info and availability. Just hit reply to coordinate.

02

Play 2 Sets

Meet up at any approved court and play 2 sets. No pressure to finish by month's end - life happens. Play when it works for both of you.

03

Report & Climb

Log your scores on the dashboard. Every game you win counts toward your total. The more you play, the higher you climb. It's that simple.
