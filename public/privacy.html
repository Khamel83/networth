Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

Information We Collect

NET WORTH Tennis collects the following information to operate the tennis ladder:

  • Name and email address (for login and communication)
  • Tennis skill level (NTRP rating)
  • Match results and scores
  • Court preferences and availability

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used exclusively for operating the tennis ladder:

  • Display your name and rank on the public ladder
  • Facilitate match scheduling between players
  • Send notifications about challenges and match results
  • Maintain accurate player statistics

Information Sharing

We respect your privacy and limit what is shared:

  • Your name, rank, and match record are publicly visible on the ladder
  • Your email address is only visible to players you challenge or who challenge you
  • We never sell or share your information with third parties for marketing
  • We do not use your data for advertising purposes

Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a trusted cloud database provider. We use industry-standard encryption for data in transit and at rest.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Withdraw from the ladder at any time

To exercise these rights, contact us at the support page.

Contact

For privacy questions or concerns, please visit our support page.
