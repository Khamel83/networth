Privacy

The short version: we're not trying to steal your data.

What We Collect

Just the basics to run the ladder:

  • Your name and email (so we can pair you up)
  • Your skill level (so matches are fair)
  • Match scores (to update the ladder)
  • When you're available to play

That's it. No tracking, no analytics, no creepy stuff.

What We Do With It

  • Show your name and games won on the ladder
  • Send you emails about your monthly match
  • Share your email with your opponent (so you can coordinate)

We don't sell your info. We don't advertise. This is just a tennis ladder, not a tech company.

Who Sees What

  • Public: Your name, rank, and total games won
  • Your opponent only: Your email and availability
  • Nobody else: Everything else

Want Out?

No problem. Just email us and we'll delete everything. No hard feelings - hope you had fun playing!

Questions? Hit up support.
