Ladder Rules

"The point is, ladies and gentleman, that greed - for lack of a better word - is good."

01 Eligibility

The NET WORTH Tennis Ladder is open to women tennis players of all skill levels in the East Side Los Angeles area.

  • Players must be 18 years or older
  • Players must have access to approved court locations
  • Players must agree to follow USTA rules of play
  • New players are placed at the bottom of the ladder

02 Challenges

Players may challenge opponents ranked above them on the ladder.

  • You may challenge players up to 3 positions above you
  • Challenges must be accepted within 7 days
  • Matches must be played within 14 days of acceptance
  • If a challenge is declined without valid reason, the challenger moves up one position

03 Match Format

All ladder matches follow standard USTA singles rules.

  • Best of 3 sets (first to 2 sets wins)
  • Tiebreaker at 6-6 in each set
  • 10-point match tiebreaker in lieu of third set (optional, if both players agree)
  • Players provide their own balls (new or like-new)

04 Ranking Points

Your position on the ladder is determined by your total points.

  • Starting score: 1200 points (new players)
  • Win: +10 points
  • Win against higher-ranked opponent: +15 points
  • Loss: -5 points
  • Forfeiting or declining without valid reason: -20 points

Important Note

Both players are responsible for reporting match scores within 48 hours. Disputed scores will be reviewed by ladder administrators. Play fair, play hard.

05 Approved Courts

Matches should be played at one of the following approved public courts:

  • Vermont Canyon Tennis Courts - Griffith Park
  • Griffith Park Tennis - Riverside Dr
  • Echo Park Recreation Center
  • Silver Lake Recreation Center
  • Elysian Park Tennis Courts
  • Los Feliz Municipal Courts

06 Sportsmanship

Good sportsmanship is expected at all times.

  • Players must make their own line calls honestly
  • Abusive language or behavior will result in automatic forfeit
  • Repeated violations may result in removal from the ladder
  • Be on time - 15 minute grace period, then forfeit
