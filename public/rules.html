How It Works

Simple monthly matches. Climb the ladder based on games won.

01 Monthly Pairings

Each month, you'll be paired with another player at a similar skill level.

  • At the start of each month, you'll receive an email with your match
  • The email includes your opponent's contact info and availability
  • Just hit reply to email your opponent directly
  • Coordinate a time that works for both of you

02 Match Format

Matches are 2 sets of singles tennis.

  • Play 2 full sets (no third set)
  • Tiebreaker at 6-6 in each set
  • Report both set scores when done
  • Players provide their own balls

03 How Ranking Works

Your position on the ladder is based on total games won - not match wins.

  • Every game you win counts toward your total
  • A 6-4, 6-3 match = 12 games for the winner, 7 for the opponent
  • Even in a loss, you earn games toward your ranking
  • Rankings update automatically after each match

Why Games Won?

This system rewards competitive play. A close 7-6, 6-7 match gives both players nearly the same points. It's not just about winning - it's about playing good tennis.

04 Reporting Your Score

After your match, report the score on the dashboard.

  • Either player can report the score
  • Enter each set score (e.g., 6-4, 3-6)
  • Let us know if you'd play this person again (helps with future pairings)
  • Matches can be completed even after the month ends

05 Taking a Break

Life happens! If you need to skip a month:

  • Mark yourself as "unavailable" in your profile before the 1st
  • You'll automatically be back in the pool next month
  • No penalty for taking breaks - we'll check in when you're back
  • Your ranking stays the same while you're away

06 Approved Courts

Play at any of these East Side LA public courts:

  • Vermont Canyon - Griffith Park
  • Griffith Park - Riverside Dr
  • Griffith Park - Merry-Go-Round
  • Echo Park
  • Hermon Park
  • Eagle Rock
  • Cheviot Hills
  • Poinsettia Park

07 Good Vibes Only

This is about having fun and meeting fellow tennis players.

  • Be on time (15 min grace period)
  • Make honest line calls
  • Be a good sport, win or lose
  • If a match isn't working out, just let us know - no drama
