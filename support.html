Frequently Asked Questions

How do I join the ladder?
Email us at matches@networthtennis.com with your name, skill level (NTRP rating), and preferred contact information. We'll add you to the ladder and send you all the details to get started.
How is my ranking determined?
Rankings are based on your match results. When you win a challenge match against a higher-ranked player, you move up to their position. New players start at the bottom of the ladder and work their way up.
How do I challenge someone?
Email matches@networthtennis.com with the name of the player you'd like to challenge (must be within 3 positions above you). We'll connect you both via email to arrange the match details.
Where are matches played?
Matches are played at public tennis courts in East Los Angeles. Popular locations include Vermont Canyon, Griffith Park (Riverside and Merry-Go-Round courts), Echo Park, Hermon Park, and Eagle Rock. See the court locations on the main ladder page with direct map links.
What if I need to cancel a match?
Provide your opponent with at least 24 hours notice. Players are allowed 1 last-minute cancellation per month. No-shows result in an automatic forfeit. Coordinate directly with your opponent and notify us at matches@networthtennis.com if needed.
How do I report match results?
The winner should email the final score to matches@networthtennis.com within 48 hours of match completion. Include both players' names and the set scores (e.g., "6-4, 6-3").
What if I'll be away for a while?
Let us know if you'll be unavailable for an extended period. You'll be moved to an "inactive" status to prevent challenge requests. You can rejoin at your previous position when you return.
Is there a membership fee?
NET WORTH Tennis Ladder is currently free to join and participate. Players are only responsible for bringing tennis balls to their matches.

Common Issues

My opponent isn't responding to my messages

If your opponent doesn't respond within 7 days of a challenge, email us and we'll follow up. Challenges must be accepted or declined within 7 days.

I disagree with a match result

Contact us immediately at matches@networthtennis.com with details about the dispute. We'll review and make a determination within 3 business days.

I can't find any available courts

Try checking the court availability early in the morning or on weekdays. You can also try less busy courts like Hermon Park or Eagle Rock. Many players successfully schedule matches for weekend mornings.

Technical issues with the website

If you're experiencing problems viewing the ladder or accessing links, please email us with details about the issue (browser, device, what you're seeing). We'll help troubleshoot and fix any technical problems.
